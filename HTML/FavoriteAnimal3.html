    The blobfish is a deep sea fish of the family Psychrolutidae. It inhabits the deep warm waters off the coasts of mainland Australia,Tasmania, and New Zealand. Blobfish are typically shorter than 30 cm long and weigh about 3 pounds. They are also known as the ugliest creatures alive.
